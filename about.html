FAQs:

Common design questions

I'm interested in collaboration. Where do I start?

We're thrilled to hear from you! Please send us a brief overview of your project to menutech@gmail.com or through our contact page. We'll respond promptly to discuss your needs further and arrange an initial meeting.

What services do you offer?

We specialize in visual identity design and website development, crafting striking brand identities, logos, websites, and marketing materials that help businesses stand out and connect with their audience.

Do you work with startups or only established businesses?

We work with both! Whether you're building a brand from scratch or refining an existing identity, we tailor our approach to meet your unique needs and goals.

How long does a branding or website project take?

Timelines vary based on project complexity, but branding typically takes 4-6 weeks, while website design and development can range from 6-10 weeks. We ensure a smooth and efficient process from start to finish.

Can I request revisions during the design process?

Absolutely. We provide multiple revision rounds to refine and perfect your brand identity or website, ensuring the final result aligns with your vision.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes! We design and develop fully responsive websites that look great and function seamlessly across all devices, including mobile, tablet, and desktop.

Do you provide ongoing support after the project is completed?

Yes, we offer maintenance, updates, and support to ensure your brand and website remain polished, secure, and up-to-date.

What makes your approach different?

We combine strategy, design, and storytelling to create visually compelling and functional brand identities. Everything we craft is custom-built, ensuring authenticity and impact.

How do I get started?

Simply reach out to us! We’ll discuss your needs, goals, and vision, then create a tailored plan to bring your brand to life.
